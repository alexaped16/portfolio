My Influences

In terms of web design, I am inspired by Memphis Design and Neo-Brutalism. Memphis design is all about creating an approachable, colorful 1980's aesthetic. Neo-brutalism references an architecture movement in the 1950's, which emphasized the raw materials of design - like concrete. If I could be like anyone, it would probably be Maya Angelou. She taught me that emotions are lovely and that it's good to laugh at yourself. I am a firm believer in having a Fika (Swedish tradition of taking time during the day to be present with a friend and drink a cup of coffee ).
